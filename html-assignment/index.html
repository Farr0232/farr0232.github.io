About Me

Driven by a passion to create and a love for capturing images, Connor Farrell brings his photographic expertise and artistic knowledge to every project. Connor is based out of Ottawa, he studied at Mohawk College in Hamilton where he furthered his talents and his knowledge. In his first semester, Connor was published as an up and coming photographer in PhotoEd's Fall 2017 Magazine. Later, he would win first place in the On Location category in Mohawk's Photo Night.

Since graduation, Connor has worked with local businesses like Beefer's Skate Shop to international companies such as Black Cellar Wine to create images for publication and social media purposes. Even though photography will always be Connor's passion, he has started branching out creating websites, graphics, and videos for businesses and companies.
